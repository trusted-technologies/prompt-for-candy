# VDS Virtual Servers — Overview and Management

Two lines: VDS PROMO and VDS HI-CPU. Both are available in Russia and Poland.
Management via SolusVM: Poland — https://solusvm.apexnodes.xyz, Russia — https://solusvm.msk.sh.
OS reinstallation: in the SolusVM panel, select the server → Reinstall → select the OS. All data will be deleted!
VDS PROMO: Intel Xeon, DDR3/DDR4, NVMe, 1 Gbps.
PROMO plans: VDS-PROMO-1 (1 vCPU, 2GB RAM, 32GB NVMe) — $1.54/month; VDS-PROMO-2 (2 vCPU, 4GB, 64GB) — $2.37; VDS-PROMO-3 (4 vCPU, 8GB, 128GB) — $4.05; VDS-PROMO-4 (8 vCPU, 16GB, 256GB) — $7.39; VDS-PROMO-5 (16 vCPU, 32GB, 512GB) — $14.08.
VDS HI-CPU: DDR5, NVMe, 1 Gbps. In Russia — AMD Ryzen 9 5900X, in Poland — AMD Ryzen 7 7700X.
HI-CPU plans: VDS-HI-CPU-1 (1 vCPU, 2GB, 32GB) — $3.32; VDS-HI-CPU-2 (2, 4GB, 64GB) — $5.64; VDS-HI-CPU-3 (4, 8GB, 128GB) — $10.28; VDS-HI-CPU-4 (8, 16GB, 256GB) — $19.56; VDS-HI-CPU-5 (16, 32GB, 512GB) — $38.12.
Prices are the same for both locations; only the processors differ in HI-CPU.
All plans include DDoS protection and One-Click Apps.
If a plan is out of stock — it is temporarily sold out, advise to follow social media. Do not promise specific timeframes.
